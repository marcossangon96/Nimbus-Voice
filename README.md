# Nimbus Voice

Nimbus Voice is an AI-powered voice assistant that lets you **type messages and hear them spoken aloud** in realistic voices.

## How It Works

1. **Type a message** in the text box.
2. **Choose a voice** from the dropdown menu.
3. **Click Send**.
4. The AI generates a response using **Vertex AI** and converts it to speech with **ElevenLabs TTS**.
5. Listen to the audio and read the text response directly in your browser.

## Features

- Real-time AI-generated responses.
- Multiple high-quality voices to choose from.
- Responsive and modern interface with pastel Material Design.
- Works entirely in the browser, no downloads required.
- Suitable for chat, storytelling, or voice demos.

## Interface Guide

- **Text Box**: Where you type your message to the AI.
- **Voice Selector**: Pick the voice you want the AI to speak with.
- **Send Button**: Sends your message to the AI and plays the audio response.
- **Audio Player**: Plays the AI-generated voice output.
- **Response Display**: Shows the text generated by the AI.

## Tips

- Keep messages short for faster responses.
- Try different voices to find the most expressive one.
- Use the scroll bar if the response text is long.

## Demo

Open the app at `https://nimbus-voice.vercel.app/` and start chatting immediately.

## Supported Browsers

- Chrome, Firefox, Edge, Safari (latest versions recommended)
- Mobile devices supported, but best experience on desktop

Nimbus Voice makes interacting with AI more natural by combining **text generation** and **realistic speech** in one seamless experience.
